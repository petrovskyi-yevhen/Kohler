SHOWERHEADS

Customize your shower with a range of showerheads, handshowers, and more, spanning traditional to contemporary styles. Traditional showerheads provide abundant, full water drops, while HydroRail® and Awaken® options offer a more adaptable showering approach.

Katalyst® air-induction technology enhances water flow for a richer, more intense sensory experience. All options are Kohler branded, guaranteeing top-notch design and functionality.

Enhance your shower experience further by combining your showerhead with a matching shower barre and glass door. Additionally, your LuxStone shower can have your showerhead installed in as little as one day.
