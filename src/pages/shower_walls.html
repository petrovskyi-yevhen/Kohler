SHOWER WALLS

Kohler's top-notch designers personally selected each design featured in our LuxStone shower walls collection to enhance your bathroom renovation.
Available in a variety of colors, patterns and combinations.
Made of crushed stone for enhanced durability.
Walls are custom-fitted based on your shower’s dimensions and installed in as little as a day.
Select the ideal color, texture, and pattern for your shower enclosure.
VeinCut Dune
Based on the unique and intricate patterns of real stone, VeinCut Dune features smooth parallel lines and a light sandy hue to give this design an organic feel. Use it as a center accent wall with White or Biscuit side walls.
CrossCut Dune
Inspired by the sprawling veins of natural stone, the white-and-gray design of CrossCut Dune adds a luxurious look to your shower walls.
White Brick
The modern industrial look of White Brick adds subtle texture to shower designs while maintaining a clean, bright look.
Calacatta Crema
Calacatta Crema bridges the gap between classic and modern aesthetics with its gray and white stone pattern, providing a harmonious touch that instills a tranquil sense of minimalism within the shower area.
Cintilante Bluette
Inspired by the Brazilian stone Cintilante Bluette, LuxStone’s boldest design features dynamic patterning in cool blue and white that makes a statement.
Ice Grey
A cool color that works well with many bold accent tones and materials. It is a soft shade of grey that acts as a neutral and a modern alternative to white.
White
Clean and crisp, White is a perfect fit for any style. This neutral works in traditional or contemporary shower designs and allows for total freedom in decorating.
Biscuit
Biscuit is a classic, creamy shade with just a hint of yellow. This very soft color is at home with contemporary as well as traditional and country settings.
